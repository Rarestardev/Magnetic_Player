🎵 About the App: MG Player (Music Player)

MG Player is a beautifully designed, fast, and intelligent audio app that elevates your music experience. With a focus on visual harmony, precise controls, and smart organization, it’s crafted for both Iranian and global users who care deeply about how music feels and flows.
